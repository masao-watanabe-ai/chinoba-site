Research Initiative

Chinoba

Intelligence as Relationship

Chinoba is a research initiative exploring intelligence as relationships between AI, humans, organizations, protocols, and society.

A platform for runtime experimentation, decision infrastructure, and structured exploration of intelligence as a relational system.

Why Chinoba Exists

AI is becoming operational.

But connecting AI to the real world remains difficult.

Chinoba explores how AI, humans, organizations, trust, and knowledge can be connected through structured decision systems.

AI is becoming operational.

Reality contains ambiguity, responsibility, and boundaries.

AI outputs are not the same as real-world decisions.

DTM introduces structure between AI and reality.

Intelligence emerges through relationships.

This is Chinoba.
