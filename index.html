Chinoba

Intelligence as Relationship

Chinoba is a research initiative exploring intelligence as relationships between AI, humans, organizations, protocols, and society.

Research Areas:

  • Decision Trace Model
  • Runtime Society
  • Knowledge Flow
  • Trust Infrastructure
  • AI Coordination
  • Multi-Agent Systems
  • Governance and Boundary Systems
